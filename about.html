About Agri-Direct

Agri-Direct is a modern agriculture marketplace designed to help farmers connect directly with buyers without intermediaries.

Our platform supports transparent pricing, fresh farm products, and a sustainable farming ecosystem powered by technology.

We aim to empower local farmers and provide customers with quality agricultural products delivered fresh from farms.

Our Mission

Support farmers with better profits, direct market access, and digital opportunities through technology-driven solutions.

Our Vision

Build a transparent and modern agriculture ecosystem accessible to every farmer and buyer across India.
